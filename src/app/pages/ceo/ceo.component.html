Dear Patrons, Partners and Artisans,

At HomeTown, we believe that every handcrafted piece carries the soul of the hands that made it. What began as a simple conviction — that the extraordinary artistry of our villages deserves a place in homes across the world — has grown into a movement to bring authentic, handmade craft and folk art to a global audience.

Our mission is twofold. First, to preserve and celebrate India's living heritage of handmade art — the Madhubani and Warli paintings, the hand-thrown terracotta, the handloom weaves and the cast-brass craft that have been passed down through generations. Second, and just as important, to empower the rural artisan. By connecting craftspeople directly to discerning customers, we are building fair, dignified and sustainable livelihoods in the very communities where this art is born — ensuring that the maker, not the middleman, is the one who prospers.

Every purchase on HomeTown is more than a transaction; it is an investment in a rural family, in the continuity of a craft tradition, and in a more equitable creative economy for our country. Behind each product is a story, an artisan, and a community we are proud to stand beside.

As we grow, our commitment remains unchanged: uncompromising quality, honest provenance, and an unwavering respect for the people who make our work possible. Thank you for being part of the HomeTown family and for helping us take the craft of our heartland to the world.
